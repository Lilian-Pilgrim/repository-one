It is great to have you visiting this site. Thank you!

This site is designed to help you find solutions for your physical, mental and spiritual health and well-being.

And, yes! You can still have a "normal" life, including your daily cup of coffe, and still feel well balanced.

As a child I remember my mother treating us for the day-to-day illness with natural remedies. If we had a cold there would be a strong tea of garlic, onion and raw honey for us to drink. Yep, no laughing matter about that one.

For the relieve of my teenage son and some of us, the alternative world has moved along way in the last 35 years or so!

Cold and flu apart, I think, more than ever, a lot of us have become more interested in the emotional and spiritual mind behind the physical symptoms or hindering behaviour we experience either within ourselves or in our loved ones.

I have specialised in Systemic Constellation with emphasis on Family Constellation, Bach Flower Essences and Coaching.

Here, you will learn how these therapies can be used in a practical away to overcome different emotions of the mind and of the spirit, which reflects on our physical body.

This will also be talking about the earthly staff. After all, we live in this beautiful planet! So, unless you are moving some other “space”, it’s helpful to learn ways to be harmony with life here.

In Life Style. you can find information from food for life to skin products for real beauty to alternative ways to elegantly revamp and recycle your wardrobe.

Whether you are battling procrastination, depression, health issues or some sort of negative repetitive patterns in your life, these therapies can help you to heal the root cause of the problem. Sometimes one may or may not be able to logically explain why or where the issue in hand comes from. All one knows is that it is stopping one to move forward with life, to be the best one can be.

That is no more!
