Who am I?

I am passionate about life and as far as I can remember I have always wanted to make the most of each day! Yes, I know some days that seems just not possible.

I am also an Alternative Therapist, with over 12 years experience, specialised in Systemic Constellations, Flower Essences, and Coaching.

My aim is to help people to find the root cause of what is preventing them to move on with life and support their healing journey back to physical, emotional and spiritual health.

I have been working in Brazil, London and from October/18 I will also working in Devon, south West of UK.
